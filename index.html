Bio

Hello!

I am currently a PhD candidate at Xi'an Jiaotong University, China. I am co-advised by the Prof. Jian Liang and Prof. Leila De Floriani.

My research focus on computer graphics with special interests in point cloud processing, polygon mesh processing, 3D human body scanning, as well as 3D optial reconstruction. My research profiles can be found at both Google Scholar and Research Gate.
